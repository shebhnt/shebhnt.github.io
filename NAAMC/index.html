The NAAMC has a simple goal: to encourage African Americans to join the harvest. Today, millions of people live and die without hearing God's good news. God is asking for people to go, will you respond with "send me?"

Mission Ministry Award

Recognizing the special challenges many missionaries, especially African Americans, face to secure funding for mission service, the NAAMC established the Mission Ministry Award (MMA). Criteria for the award is attached to the application. Deadline for submission of applications is May 31, 2017. Applications should be returned to info@thenaamc.org.
