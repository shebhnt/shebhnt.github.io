FAQ

About Us

What is AVOH?

AVOH is a mobile friendly real estate website (professional digital billboard) and branding platform, designed for Realtors who want to stay up-to-date with their clients on every platform. AVOH stands for All Valleys Online Homes.

How long have we been in buisness?

AVOH was created in 2014. Mark, one of the owners, has been doing web development since 1996.

Websites

How much does it cost?

If you are a SRAR member, AVOH websites are only $9.95 a month, no contracts. Through technology we have been enabled to allow for low price, cost effective websites.

Is there a contract?

No! We do not want our users to be locked into anything, so all of our website plans are contract free. You can try and leave anytime you want!

Can I use my existing domain name?

Yes! We can point your existing domain name to your new site, or help you purchase a new domain.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up for a website today at this page.

Can I put my listings on my AVOH website?

Your AVOH website will be equipped with the option to add your featured listings whether its sold or listed on the market to advertise.

What if I needed to change something?

Your AVOH website will have a friendly admin panel for you to customize pictures, text, listings, and much more!

Does it have a blog? Am I going to have to post on it?

Your website will have a blog system that we keep updated for you! Making you look good to potential or current clients and showing them you are active in the real estate market world. The posts will be made to look like they are posted by you!

Can I link my facebook?

Yes you can! When enabled, every time we make a blog post on your site it will also be sent automatically to your Facebook!

Is there a home search?

Yes, every website is equipped with a full home search that is always connected to you. If someone favorites a home, you are notified. You are always in control when the user is searching and they won't be tempted to use another realtor.
