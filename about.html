About Me

It all started a long time ago when I was just a speck in my father's eye... wait, that may be too much context, let me try this again...

My name is Shelbey Hunt (yeah this is a much better start) and I am a media swiss army knife. I have a film degree from Biola University, have been the media director for Grace Chapel, and am currently serving as a missionary with SIM International Leadership and Services as a designer and technical analyst.

When I left college, I had a plan to be a "missionary to hollywood" which is a nice Christian way of saying I wanted to be rich and famous. God didn't share this vision and He quickly showed that the castles I was building were built on sand. And from the ruins God helped me start to build on solid ground.

I was given a passion, to use the skills God had given me to help build His kingdom around the world. I now seek to use my skills wherever God calls me.
