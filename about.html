About Me

Some quick facts about me: I am a Christian, I am an American, I have lived in 2 countries, I have visited at least 17 countries, I can do card tricks, I love games, I own two cats, I have been married for 7 years, and I love to create.

I have been on a quest to create things since I was little. This has led me to try everything from design, to programming, to film, to writing, to web, to screen printing, and more. If there is a way to create, I have tried, or want to try, it.

My other big motivation behind what I do is games. I love any type of game. I see everything I do as a game, and I’m always trying to figure out what is the best strategy to win.

These two core attributes have led me to be able to handle quite a variety of projects. I can be both the leader or a worker. I can be both the person coming up with the concept or the person who makes the concept a reality. I have worked in fast paced environments, relaxed environments, where I am the entire team, where I am working with people from around the world, under the pressure of deadlines, and from an office/home/airport.

I also love to share ideas with others. This has led to leading trainings both one on one and with large groups, speaking opportunities at conferences and churches, and pitching ideas to individuals or committees.

I truly am a “jack of all trades” and I can fit into almost any role.
